Lab Director

Dr. Rafia Mumtaz

Dr. Rafia Mumtaz received her Ph.D. in 2010 from the University of Surrey, UK, in the area of Remote Sensing and Satellite image processing. She is currently working as tenure Associate Professor and the Director Internet of Things (IoT) Lab, at NUST-SEECS. She is the recipient of several national and international research grants worth PKR 33 million. She has been awarded NUST-SEECS Best Researcher Award in the year 2019.
